What are Floating Keez?

Floating Keez are 10,002 top-secret alien cloning projects that were supposed to be sent to original aliens that live on the planet Keeloanus by Earth scientists. But they got caught by the government so the scientists had to send the capsule unexpectedly to space. The capsule exploded in space and now all the KeeZ are floating in space. By minting Floating Keez you will help teleport KeeZ to the planet Keeloanus .
